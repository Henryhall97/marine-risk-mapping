Subject: Permission Request — Use of NOAA Fisheries Species Illustrations in Non-Commercial Marine Conservation Platform

Dear NOAA Fisheries Web Publishing Team,

I am writing to request permission to use cetacean species profile illustrations from the NOAA Fisheries Species Directory (fisheries.noaa.gov/species-directory/marine-mammals) on a non-commercial marine conservation and citizen science platform called WhaleWatch.

**About the project:**
WhaleWatch is an open-source, non-commercial research platform (https://github.com/Henryhall97/marine-risk-mapping) that maps whale–vessel collision risk across US waters using AIS vessel traffic data, OBIS cetacean sightings, NOAA ship strike records, bathymetry, and ocean covariates. The platform includes a citizen science component where members of the public can report whale sightings to improve collision risk models.

**How the illustrations would be used:**
The platform includes a species identification wizard that helps users identify cetacean species when submitting sighting reports. Your 640×427 profile illustrations (24 species, covering baleen whales, toothed whales, dolphins, and porpoises) are anatomically accurate and visually consistent — far superior to any alternatives for educational species identification. We would display them within the identification wizard alongside text-based field marks to help users distinguish between species.

**Specific illustrations requested:**
24 illustrations from the Species Directory, including:
- Baleen whales: North Atlantic Right Whale, Humpback, Blue, Fin, Sei, Minke, Gray, Bowhead, Bryde's
- Toothed whales: Sperm Whale, Beluga, Narwhal
- Dolphins: Killer Whale (Orca), Bottlenose, Common, Spotted, Striped, White-sided, Risso's, Pilot Whale
- Porpoises: Harbor Porpoise, Dall's Porpoise, Vaquita

**Attribution:**
Full credit is provided on the platform's attribution page (whalewatch.org/attribution) reading:
"Cetacean species illustrations sourced from NOAA Fisheries Species Directory. General credit: NOAA Fisheries. Select illustrations: Jack Hornady / NOAA Fisheries."

**Terms of use:**
- Non-commercial, open-source educational/conservation project
- Images are not redistributed as standalone files — they are displayed within the platform UI only
- No modifications to the illustrations themselves (we add only a species name text label at the bottom)
- We are happy to adjust attribution or usage per any requirements you specify

I understand that NOAA Fisheries grants permission to use graphics on a case-by-case basis, and that some illustrations may be contractor-created works with different licensing terms. I am happy to provide any additional information needed.

Thank you for your time and for the exceptional quality of these educational resources.
