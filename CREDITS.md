## US Government Data (Public Domain)

Works produced by the US Federal Government are in the **public domain**
under 17 U.S.C. § 105. Courtesy citations are provided below.

### MarineCadastre AIS Vessel Traffic
- **Source:** [MarineCadastre.gov](https://marinecadastre.gov/) — joint
  Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (BOEM) / NOAA initiative.
- **License:** Public Domain (US Government work).
- **Citation:** AIS data provided by MarineCadastre.gov, a joint BOEM/NOAA
  initiative. Accessed 2024.

### NOAA Ship Strike Records
- **Source:** [NOAA InPort Record 23127](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/inport/item/23127)
- **License:** Public Domain (US Government work).
- **Citation:** Jensen, A.S. & Silber, G.K. Large Whale Ship Strike
  Database. NOAA Technical Memorandum NMFS-OPR. InPort Record 23127.

### NOAA Marine Protected Areas Inventory
- **Source:** [NOAA MPA Inventory](https://marineprotectedareas.noaa.gov/)
- **License:** Public Domain (US Government work).
- **Citation:** NOAA National Marine Protected Areas Center. MPA Inventory
  (2023). https://marineprotectedareas.noaa.gov/

### Seasonal Management Areas (SMAs)
- **Source:** 50 CFR § 224.105 — Right Whale Ship Strike Reduction.
- **License:** Public Domain (US Federal regulation).
- **Citation:** NOAA Fisheries, Seasonal Management Areas for North
  Atlantic Right Whale Ship Strike Reduction, 50 CFR § 224.105.

### NOAA CetMap Biologically Important Areas
- **Source:** [NOAA CetMap](https://cetsound.noaa.gov/biologically-important-areas)
- **License:** Public Domain (US Government work).
- **Citation:** NOAA Fisheries, Cetacean & Sound Mapping — Biologically
  Important Areas. CetMap. Accessed 2024.

### NMFS Critical Habitat Designations
- **Source:** [NOAA Fisheries Critical Habitat](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/national/endangered-species-conservation/critical-habitat)
- **License:** Public Domain (US Government work).
- **Citation:** NOAA Fisheries, ESA Critical Habitat Designations for
  Whale Species. NMFS MapServer. Accessed 2024.

### NOAA Coast Survey Shipping Lanes
- **Source:** [NOAA Office of Coast Survey](https://nauticalcharts.noaa.gov/)
- **License:** Public Domain (US Government work).
- **Citation:** NOAA Office of Coast Survey, Marine Transportation
  data. Shipping lanes, traffic separation schemes, and precautionary
  areas. Accessed 2024.

### Right Whale Slow Zones / Dynamic Management Areas
- **Source:** [NOAA Fisheries Right Whale Slow Zones](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/feature-story/help-slow-down-north-atlantic-right-whales)
- **License:** Public Domain (US Government work).
- **Citation:** NOAA Fisheries, Right Whale Vessel Strike Reduction

### NOAA OISST v2.1 (Sea Surface Temperature)
- **Source:** NOAA ERDDAP
- **License:** Public Domain (US Government work).
- **Citation:** Huang, B., et al. (2021). Improvements of the Daily
  Optimum Interpolation Sea Surface Temperature (DOISST) Version 2.1.
  *Journal of Climate*, 34(8), 2923–2939.
  https://doi.org/10.1175/JCLI-D-20-0166.1

## Commercial Use Restrictions Summary

The following data sources have **non-commercial use** restrictions:

| Source | Restriction |
|---|---|
| Watkins WMMSDB (whale audio) | Personal and academic purposes only |
| Happywhale Kaggle (whale photos) | Competition, academic, non-commercial only |

If this project is used for commercial purposes, the photo and audio
classifiers must be retrained on differently-licensed data.
